about

A resourceful, self-motivated and results-driven Web Designer / Front-End Developer with a passion for creating websites and web applications that are easy to navigate, aesthetically appealing, responsive, reliable and secure.

In addition to my love of web design and development, I am also interested in a range of integrated digital marketing services and search technologies in order to help costumers get more reach and consequently boost their online audience.

You can find more about my skills, experience and education on my resume.

As you can see, I have over ten years of work experience as a QS in the UK. Working in office and on site in previous positions, I managed all the costs relating to construction projects. From the initial calculations to the final figures, I minimized the costs and enhanced value for money, while still achieving the required standards and quality. Additionally, I always tried to ensure projects were completed within the budget and on time.

While I have enjoyed being a Design Technician and a QS for the past fifteen years, I was made redundant twice in my career. Being the first time during the 2008-2009 financial crisis and the second during the 2020-2021 pandemic which prompted me to consider a career switch in order to have more stability in my life.

Hence, the start of my new journey into the IT industry in December 2021 and since then I have never looked back.
